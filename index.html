Invoice risk scoring + fraud detection for deferred invoices

Invoice RiskLens does not pay invoices. It is an infrastructure layer that produces risk scores and detects potential fraud / fake invoicing. The payer is a bank, factoring company, fund, or Islamic bank.

How it works

1) SME uploads a deferred invoice

2) RiskLens scores risk and flags fraud signals

3) Financial institution reviews the score

4) If approved, the institution funds the invoice
